Our robot

It is designed to pick up the cargo balls and deposit them into the cargo bay.

We decided to incorporate the use of compliant wheels, which are very squishy rubber wheels. If we spun them fast enough, they could push the ball into the compartment and hold it there. If we spun the wheels the other way, the ball would be spat out.
