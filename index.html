Who are we?

We are a group of highschool students from Reynolds Secondary who are interested in the fields of Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics.

We started this team at the beginning of this school year with the intentions of competing in the FIRST Robotics Competition.

Our robot

It is designed to pick up the cargo balls and deposit them into the cargo bay.

As a rookie team, we wanted to not focus on every single aspect of the competition, but be really good at one part.

We decided to incorporate the use of compliant wheels, which are very squishy rubber wheels. If we spun them fast enough, they could push the ball into the compartment and hold it there. If we spun the wheels the other way, the ball would be spat out.
