About us

The Reynolds Reybots are a FIRST Robotics team from Reynolds Secondary School, Greater Victoria School District 61. The team was founded by a group of students who were passionate in STEM and interested in starting a robotics team with the intention to compete in the FIRST Robotics Competition. As a rookie team, the Reynolds Reybots hopes to learn from other more experienced teams in the competition as well as foster the interest of science, technology, engineering and mathematics in their community.
