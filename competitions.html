FIRST Championship

Deep Space - 2019 - Houston, TX

Our first competition as a rockie team in which we won our Rockie Award.

Our robot was designed to pick up the cargo balls and deposit them into the the cargo bay.

We decided to incorporate the use of compliant wheels. If we spuned them fast enough, they could push the ball into the compartment and hold it there. If we spun the wheels the other way, the ball would be spat out.
